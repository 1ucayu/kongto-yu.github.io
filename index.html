Luca Jiangtao Yu     于江涛  （于江濤）

I am especially interested in multi- modality/sensor machine learning and wireless AI perception, including signal processing, acoustic sensing, and some novel works combining various modalities.

Currently, I am an incoming 4-year Ph.D at The University of Hong Kong majoring in Computer Science and a senior at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications majoring in Information Engineering. Now I am a student at AIoT Lab, Department of CS, HKU, advised by Prof. Chenshu Wu and a research intern at MARS Lab, Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Hang Zhao. Previously, I used to be an research intern at Intelligent Communication Software & Multimedia Key Lab ,School of CS, BUPT, advised by Prof. Anfu Zhou.
