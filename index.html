Jiangtao Yu     于江涛  （於江濤）

I am currently an incoming 4-year Ph.D in The University of Hong Kong and a senior in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications majoring in Information Engineering. Now I am a student in AIoT Lab, advised by Prof. Chenshu Wu at Dept. of CS, HKU and a research assistant in MARS Lab, advised by Prof. Hang Zhao at IIIS, Tsinghua University. I am also used to be an intern in The Intelli. Comm. Software & Multi-media Key Lab of Beijing at School of CS, BUPT, advised by Prof. Anfu Zhou.

I am especially interested in multi-modal and multi-sensor machine learning and wireless AI perception, including signal processing, speech processing, and some novel & interesing works combining audio, vision, language and other modalities.

My research vision is that more AI techs can empower the field of wireless perception and communication.
