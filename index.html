Hey! I am Luca Jiangtao Yu, a Ph.D. student at AIoT Lab, Department of CS, HKU, advised by Prof. Chenshu Wu. Previously, I was a research intern at MARS Lab, IIIS, THU, advised by Prof. Hang Zhao. Before that, I was a research intern at Intelligent Comm. Software and Multimedia Key Lab, BUPT, advised by Prof. Anfu Zhou.

I am especially interested in multi-modality/sensor machine learning and wireless AI perception, including signal processing, acoustic sensing, and novel works combining various modalities.
